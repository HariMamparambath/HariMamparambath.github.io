Cookie Cats: paywall gate placement test

Tested whether moving a mobile game's paywall from level 30 to level 40 would increase player retention, using a chi-square test across 90K+ players.

Finding: Moving the gate to level 40 significantly lowered 7-day retention (p = 0.0016) — recommended keeping the gate at level 30
